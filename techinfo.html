How The Benue Tech Skills Program had Helped Me






The Benue Tech Skill Program Created for The Benue Youth by the Distinguished Governor of his excellency Government Hycinth Alia Has helped me alot in ways i can't comprehend.


First of all, the Program is created to empower youths in ICT knowlege. This is of big help because, in our modern world where everything is technologically based, having ICT knowlege is very beneficial to the society.


Secondly, the program is set to prepare Benue youths in ICT knowlege and also make Job available for those who makes remarkable progress. In our country today, having such possibility means alot because securing employment in various organisation is a challenge today, but our distinguished Governor has made is possible for us to make a living after completing this Wonderful


Thirdly, the program is set to commence in two batches and this has not only helped me but has also helped my loved ones to partake in it in the next batch and also secure future benefits.


Lastly the Team and co-ordinators of this program are also not excluded. They worked hard everyday to get the learners to learn and understand what the learn. They explain some wordings in basic forms so that non ICT Illiterate will understand and not be confused.




The Benue Tech Skills Program Has helped me as well as other people in unemaginable ways in this our current times and we pray for long health and prosperity for our well distinguished governor to keep up the good work God has sent him to do.
